## Simple Heatmap with <code>imagesc</code>


<pre class="mcode">
size = 50;
z = zeros(size, size);
for r = 1:size
    for c = 1:size
        z(r,c) = r+c;
    end
end
fig = figure;

colormap('hot');
imagesc(z);
colorbar;

fig2plotly(fig);

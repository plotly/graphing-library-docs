## Graphing Network Graphs with Adjacency Matrices


<pre class="mcode">
    [B, V] = bucky;
    H = sparse(60, 60);
    k = 31:60;
    H(k, k) = B(k, k);
    
    fig = figure;
    gplot(B - H, V, 'b-');
    hold on;
    
    gplot(H, V, 'r-');
    axis off equal;
    
    fig2plotly(fig);
</pre>

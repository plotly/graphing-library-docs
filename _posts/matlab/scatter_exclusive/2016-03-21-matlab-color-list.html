--- name: Specifying Color List using Hold On plot_url: https://plotly.com/~tarzzz/2052 arrangement: horizontal language: matlab suite: scatter_exclusive order: 4 sitemap: false --- X = [linspace(0,100,100); linspace(0,100,100); linspace(0,100,100)] Y = [randi(100,1,100); randi(100,1,100); randi(100,1,100)] colors = ['r' 'g' 'b'] fig = figure; hold on for i=1:3 disp(colors(i)); scatter(X(i, :), Y(i, :),colors(i)); end; hold off resp = fig2plotly(fig, 'strip',false)
